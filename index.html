About the Red Hat design system

Our Red Hat design system's purpose is to represent the Red Hat brand digitally, helping to create a consistent experience for users across marketing, products and support. Our foundations are shared with PatternFly, an open source design system that feeds the user interfaces of our enterprise applications and beyond.
